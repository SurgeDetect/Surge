# Breakout Filter

Surge should favor continuation setups that still have room to travel after the first visible move. Not every fast candle deserves escalation.

## Favor a setup when

- Breadth expands with the move instead of narrowing.
- Depth thins in the direction of travel.
- Refills are slow or defensive rather than immediately absorptive.

## Deprioritize when

- The move depends on one isolated sweep.
- Refill quality improves too quickly against the move.
- Nearby levels show resting liquidity strong enough to trap late entries.

## Operator note

The best candidates usually show pressure, participation, and weak resistance at the same time. One of the three on its own is rarely enough.
